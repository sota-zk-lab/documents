# Schwartz-Zippel Lemma

**Lemma:**
Let $F$ be any field, and let g: $F^m → F$ be a nonzero $m$-variate polynomial of total degree (a.k.a the number of
possible roots) at most $d$. Then on any finite set $S \subseteq F$ :

$$
\begin{aligned}
Pr_{x←S^m} [g(x) = 0] ≤ d/|S|.
\end{aligned}
$$
